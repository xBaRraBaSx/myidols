____________EXO

EXO (엑소) is a South Korean boy group that currently consists of:
Suho, Xiumin, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai, and Sehun. On April 8, 2022
Lay announced his departure from SM Ent., no details regarding his possible future
activities as an EXO member have been given. Former members: Kris, Tao and Luhan.
EXO debuted on April 8, 2012, under SM Entertainment.
